Built to help you

Stillwood, since 2019

We wanted to make places that felt like an exhale.

Stillwood began with one small cabin and a simple conviction: the best kind of luxury is having less asked of you. No packed itinerary. No performance. Just careful shelter in a place worth noticing.

Quiet by design

Every room, view, and material is chosen to settle the senses rather than compete for attention.

Lightly on the land

We build small, source locally where possible, and contribute to the protection of the landscapes around us.

Warmly hosted

Thoughtful guidance before arrival, privacy while you stay, and real help nearby whenever it is needed.

“A place should not distract you
from your life. It should return you to it.”

— Elin Hart, founder
